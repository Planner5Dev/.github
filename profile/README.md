## 🧠 Overview

**Planner 5D** is an intuitive home design tool that enables you to create detailed and realistic floor plans, design interiors, and visualize your dream home in 3D. Whether you’re an amateur or a professional designer, Planner 5D provides a user-friendly interface to design, plan, and decorate your spaces with ease.

With Planner 5D, you can design rooms, add furniture, experiment with different layouts, and see how your designs will look in a 3D space. The software’s drag-and-drop functionality lets you easily place items in your design and modify them to suit your needs. It also offers a vast library of furniture, decor items, and materials to help bring your vision to life.

Planner 5D is designed for users who want to plan their interiors, gardens, and even entire homes without requiring any prior design experience. The program also allows for 3D walkthroughs, so you can explore your designs from every angle and ensure everything is just as you imagined.
